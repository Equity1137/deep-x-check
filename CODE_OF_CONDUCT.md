# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
- **Be respectful** of different viewpoints
- **Focus on constructive criticism**
- **Use inclusive language**
- **Respect privacy** - never share personal information
- **Act in good faith**

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, insults, or discrimination
- Publishing others' private information
- Trolling, spamming, or off-topic comments
- Any unethical use of the tool

## Enforcement
Violations may result in:
- Warning
- Temporary ban
- Permanent ban

## Reporting
Report violations by opening an Issue with "[CoC]" in the title.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces and public spaces when representing the project.
